Why I built FlowTask

I built FlowTask after feeling overwhelmed by productivity tools that demanded constant attention. I wanted something quieter — a tool that respects energy, allows rest, and focuses on progress without pressure. FlowTask is my attempt to build software that works with humans, not against them.
